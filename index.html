Secretary Generals' Greeting

Secretary Generals Arshita Lakhani
Dhruv Toprani
"Diplomacy is the ability to tell someone to go to hell in such a way that they ask for the directions."It is with great pleasure that we welcome you to DXBMUN 2023 by the Indian High School, Dubai. Having seen the crests and troughs of our Model UN careers together, it is our combined privilege to serve as your Secretary-Generals. The DXBMUN Team is working tirelessly to bring you a conference of a lifetime. Therefore, it is with great excitement, we officially begin the countdown to DXBMUN 2023. The organizing committee strives to maintain the illustrious history of our prestigious institution, promote the idea of growth through diplomacy, and provide international exposure to all delegates. Model UN as a community has grown exponentially over the past few years. A platform where people are dedicated to the continuous pursuit of growth, and the idea of fraternity amidst competition. We cannot wait for you to embark upon your MUN journey and be a part of this one of a kind experience. And for those who've done this before, hold on to your placards because this is going to be one hell of a ride!
