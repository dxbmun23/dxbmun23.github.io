Meet the Secretariat!

Arshita Lakhani

Secretary General

Hello everyone! I'm Arshita and I will be serving as your secretary-general for the duration of this conference. Looking back to my childhood, I used to be that one kid who never talked in class or raised my hand even though I knew the answer very well. I always had a lot to say but didn't have the courage to say it. My journey to where I am today began with my teacher forcing me to join my school's MUN club. What was initially a means to impress my teacher, soon became an integral part of my life. As much as I enjoy social nights and making new friends, what I really love about MUNs is the sense of accomplishment I feel when I can think on my feet and make my own decisions. With that, I hope all of you get to have an unforgettable experience at our conference. Can't wait to see you there!

Dhruv Toprani

Secretary General

There is more to see than can ever be seen and more to do than can ever be done. What's important is to make every moment of whatever you do unforgettable. I'm Dhruv Toprani and I'll be your Secretary General for DXBMUN'23. I've been doing Model UNs for about 5 years. Although every time I look back, seldom do I remember the witty comebacks, the perfectly formatted resolutions, or the glory of all the awards I picked up along the way. What I remember the most is all the remarkable people I've crossed paths with and the memories I've made. No matter where you are on your journey, an unforgettable adventure awaits you!

Baden Paiva

Deputy Secretary General

Hi, I'm Baden and I'll be serving as the Deputy Secretary General for the duration of the conference. I like politics and debates and naturally MUN has become a favorite hobby of mine. In specific world history has always fascinated me; even though to most it may seem old and boring, to me it's symbolic of how humans have been able to overcome any problem, persevere, and develop. The real strength behind the success of our civilization has not only been our (on average) larger minds but our ability to communicate and cooperate. To me, the very spirit of MUN is not only the awards or the speaking but rather the friends we make along the way. Truly MUN teaches us that friendship really is magic.

Krithi Reddy

Director General

What if I told you that the world was to end some 30 years ago? That the Cuban Missile crisis hadn't ended the way it actually did; that somehow diplomacy failed and a nuclear war started? It sounds like an apocalyptic universe straight out of a novel, only, it would be true if diplomacy hadn't displayed it's power and let peace triumph. My warmest greetings to each of you! I am Krithi Reddy .K, the Director General of DXBMUN'23. With the aim of celebrating diplomacy and all its successes, DXBMUN'23 is all set to welcome you to one of the best conferences to have existed. And with that it gives me great pleasure to invite all those budding changemakers, diplomats and future presidents for 3 days of extensive learning, debating and most importantly, changemaking.

USG Admin

Hi, I am Eshal and I will be serving as the USG of Administration for this edition of DXBMUN.
