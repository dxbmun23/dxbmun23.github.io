Meet the Secretariat!

Eshal Ajmal

Secretary General

Hi, I am Eshal and I will be serving as the Secretary General for this edition of DXBMUN. I am someone who greatly enjoys the platform a MUN conference provides to discuss world issues that may not be acceptable in other social settings, and the opportunity it provides to create wide networks and make connections with people outside of my comfort zone. After attending over 15 conferences, I have come to realize that MUNs are not just about debating and arguing on an agenda, but instead, it's more about arriving at solutions for global issues. I am sincerely looking forward to reading feasible resolutions and experiencing controversial debates. I hope that all of you will take home valuable lessons, good memories, and new friends.
